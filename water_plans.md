## Introduction

Water is the amongst the first and most important resources to be secured in an off-grid or homesteading situation.

### Calculating Water Needs

The first step in planning for water is to calculate how much water you will need / use on a month-month basis. The average American household uses 2,000 - 8,000 liters of water per month.

This means, roughly 24,000 - 96,000 liters of water per year.

This is the household water usage, and does not include any water used for livestock, gardening, or other purposes.
